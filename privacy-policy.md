**Privacy Policy for WineCellar**

Last updated: March 2026

WineCellar is an application designed to help users manage their wine cellar and interact with friends around shared bottles.

## Information We Collect

WineCellar may collect the following types of information:

* **Account information**: such as email address and username when you create an account.
* **User content**: information you add to the application such as bottles in your cellar or interactions with friends.
* **Technical data**: device information and application logs used to improve the stability and performance of the application.

## How We Use Information

The information collected is used to:

* Provide and maintain the application's features
* Allow users to manage their wine cellar
* Enable social interactions between users
* Improve the application's performance and reliability

## Camera Permission

The application may request access to the device camera. This permission is used only for features related to the application, such as scanning or capturing information related to wine bottles. The camera is never used without the user’s action.

## Data Storage

Some data is securely stored using services provided by **Google Firebase** to ensure proper functioning of the application.

## Data Sharing

WineCellar does not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except when required for the operation of the application services.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information and ensure the security of the application.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact the developer.
